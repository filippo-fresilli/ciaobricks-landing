FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Do I need original LEGO® bricks? +

No. You can choose original LEGO® bricks or generic/unbranded ones: the parts list adapts automatically to your choice.

Which devices does it work on? +

ciaobricks is a native app for iPhone and iPad running a recent version of iOS/iPadOS.

Are my photos safe? +

Image processing happens in the app. See the Privacy Policy for details on how data is handled.

How do I buy the bricks? +

The app generates the full parts list with quantities, colours and BrickLink IDs, plus a price estimate: you have everything to order them.

Which languages is it available in? +

Italian, English and French.
